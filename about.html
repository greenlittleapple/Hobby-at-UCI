What is Hobby@UCI?

General

Hobby@UCI is the first ever club at UCI dedicated to learning new hobbies! Every month, members take up a designated hobby and improve it over the month. The club provides all the resources needed for the hobby, and the only thing a member needs to do is practice it. We also have a club room (ICS 195) for the sole purpose of hobbyism, and it is open daily for members to practice together!


Monthly Process

- At the start of the month, the hobby is announced on this website and social media.
- On the first Friday of the month, we convene in the club room and the board distributes any necessary instructions / materials.
- Members can then practice the hobby with others in the club room daily or during Friday meetings, where we can report our progress.
- Finally, on the last Friday, we reflect on our total progress in a conclusion meeting and preview the next month!


Background

Hobby@UCI was started in 2018 by Marcus Wong as a way to encourage himself and others to take advantage of life. As a college student, Marcus felt that he was spending every day stuck in a boring, unhealthy routine of classes and video games. Even though he had interesting days every now and then, he felt that he was living by the day and had no long-term goals. So, he decided to create a club with the main theme of manageable goals, that would also serve as a place to hang out and learn with others!
